YouTube Collaborations

I love collaborating with other YouTubers, but I get a lot of requests to do this and cannot accept them all. So, I have a few prerequisites...

  • Have a decent Microphone
  • Have a channel focused on Zelda / Nintendo
  • Have at least 100 Subscribers (I want to make sure you’re experienced)
  • Have an idea for a video
  • Be mature

… If you meet these requirements, then heck yah! Let's talk about doing a collab! Just be sure to specify if you want the collaboration to ONLY be for your channel, or if you expect both of us to make a video for this collaboration. For instance, if your microphone is muffled or fuzzy then I won't let you do voiceovers on MY channel, but I might still do a voiceover on YOUR channel.
