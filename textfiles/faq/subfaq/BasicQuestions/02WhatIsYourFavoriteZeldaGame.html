What is your favorite Zelda game?

Current Answer: Link's Crossbow Training

Cue My classic speech about the 1-to-1 motion controls.



Old Answer: I can't decide!

My first Zelda game was Twilight Princess. I have played it more times than any other game in the series. So I love it A LOT... and yet I feel like it's missing something. And it's quite linear.

Meanwhile, Breath of the Wild and Tears of the Kingdom are SUPER open ended video games. You can go anywhere and do anything you want from the start of the game. But both games lack the item progression system I love and have smaller (or fewer) dungeons.

So let me put it this way. . .

The goal of a video game is to be fun and each Zelda game provides fun in a different way. So, depending on the mood I am in, my "favorite" Zelda game will change.

I know it’s not the answer you were looking for, but it is honestly how I feel.

Ocarina of Time is fun for it's retro feel, solid dungeons, and classic items.

Twilight Princess is fun for the dramatic artstyle, having fast paced combat, and epic bosses.

Breath of the Wild is fun for it’s exploration and physics system.

And the 2D games are fun in a COMPLETELY different way from everything else.

All the games are great in one way or another. So that is my answer.
