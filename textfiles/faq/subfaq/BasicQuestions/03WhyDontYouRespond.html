Why don't you respond to my comments anymore?

Unfortunately, not anymore. When my channel had 900 subs I was able to do that. That is why I used to say “I respond to every comment,” in my old videos. But during February 2020 my channel exploded from 900 subs to 18,000 subs. After that I could no longer reply to every comment since there were too many to keep up with.

It breaks my heart and I wish it were possible for me to talk to everyone. The best I can do is apologize to anyone whose comments I have missed.

I made an update video about it if you’re curious.
