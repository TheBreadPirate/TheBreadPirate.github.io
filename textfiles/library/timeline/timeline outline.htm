2017

Created Enclave News Network - Jan 21

Whew… this one will take a while to explain. I’ll be brief.

In 2017, I joined the Fallout New Vegas Multiplayer community. Members could join various factions, each with their own Discord. I chose The Enclave since I thought they were cool (even though they are technically evil in the lore). 

 

A few weeks after joining The Enclave I made Enclave News Network (ENN) which was a parody of Enclave Radio. I went over news in the community and propaganda related to The Enclave. Basically it was roleplay! It was a blast but the community died only 5 months after I joined.

 

I should make a video about it someday.
