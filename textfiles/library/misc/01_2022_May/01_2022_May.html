I'm Back Baby: The Bread Pirate's Summer Plans Blog

Tuesday May 10th, 2022

Hey there nerds! It's great to be back.

If couldn't already tell, I have been taking a break from YouTube. THREE MONTHS to be exact! During that time, I focused on my college work, made a video for a talent show, learned web designing, and caught up on some video games I really wanted to play...

And now I am back!

This summer, I will be making Zelda videos for YouTube full-time! However, they will be a little bit different than before.

Last summer, I started an experiment to see what would happen if I made SUPER short videos (about 1-2 minutes) instead of long videos. My hypothesis was that shorter videos would be easier to make and that people would binge several of them at a time. AND! If people binged a lot of videos, then it would generate more views. But, what really happened was thisâ€¦

…each of the videos underperformed! Not only that, but they stopped generating a meaningful amount of views after only two weeks.

What the heck! That was exactly the opposite of what I was looking for!

Okay, so what am I supposed to do now?

I WILL TELL YOU WHAT I’M GOING TO DO! I am going to go back to basics. This summer, I am going to make normal length videos (4-8 minutes).

Aside from that, do not worry. The channel will still cover Zelda Fun Facts and will continue to do so into the future.

On top of that, I want to experiment with skit videos. They might be inspired by a certain YouTuber named Begula…

Hopefully this answered most of your questions about the future of the channel. I will post more updates on the blog at least once every month.

Stay tuned, and until I see you next time; have fun storming the castle!
